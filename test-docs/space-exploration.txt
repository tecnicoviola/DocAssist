# MISSION LOG: PROJECT ARTEMIS V
Location: Mars, Ares Base 4
Commander: Elena Rostova

## Status Update
The primary habitation module has been successfully pressurized. The atmospheric scrubbers are operating at 94% efficiency. However, we have detected a minor leak in the secondary airlock seal. 

## Scientific Discoveries
During the excursion to Sector 7-G, Dr. Vance discovered a unique crystalline structure buried beneath the regolith. Initial spectroscopy suggests the presence of trapped liquid water isotopes dating back 3 billion years. This confirms the "Oasis Hypothesis".

- Conduct a full diagnostic on the rover's port-side suspension strut.
