# Grandma's Secret Recipe Book

## The Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookie
This recipe has been passed down for 4 generations. It yields 24 perfect cookies.

### Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup unsalted butter (browned, this is crucial!)
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
- THE SECRET INGREDIENT: 1 teaspoon of finely ground espresso powder. This enhances the chocolate flavor without making it taste like coffee.

### Instructions
1. Brown the butter in a saucepan over medium heat until it smells nutty and has golden brown flecks. Let it cool for 15 minutes.
2. Whisk the browned butter with both sugars until smooth.
3. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
4. Fold in the dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, and the secret espresso powder).
5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
6. Chill the dough in the fridge for exactly 2 hours. Do not skip this step!
7. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.

## Preparation Tasks
- Buy unsalted butter and chocolate chips from the store.
- Grind the espresso beans.
- Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
